BATTLESHIP

A game of naval warfare


Instructions:

**Click New Game** ADMIRAL 1 will always go first.

You each have five ships of varying lengths, a carrier (5 cells), a cruiser (4 cells), a battleship (3 cells), and a patrol boat (2 cells)

The fleet will be placed randomly around the board, and they can be horizontal or vertical.

Play begins with ADMIRAL 1 firing their first shot at a cell they think contains an enemy vessel. A HIT or a MISS will be recorded.

ADMIRAL 2 will then go, and play is repeated in an alternating fashion.

The first to destroy all ENEMY ships WINS!
